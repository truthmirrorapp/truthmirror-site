About

The story behind the mirror.

TruthMirror was built to explore the space between identity, emotion, and artificial intelligence. Not to comfort. Not to flatter. But to reflect.

Mission

Most apps are built to make you feel better. TruthMirror is built to make you see clearly — a mirror honest enough to show the patterns you spend your life avoiding, and steady enough to hold you to changing them.

Why TruthMirror exists

People rarely fail for lack of information. They fail because they rationalize, blame, intellectualize, and quietly negotiate with themselves until nothing moves. TruthMirror exists for the moment after the excuse — the question you'd rather not answer.

What makes it different

It doesn't give advice. It asks the question underneath your answer. Where other tools hand you a plan, TruthMirror hands you back your own pattern — named, tracked, and impossible to unsee. Over time it remembers what you said and refuses to let you tell the same story twice.

How we think about AI

We believe AI can be more than a tool. It can be a mirror. We use it not to predict what you want to hear, but to reflect what you actually do. The intelligence serves one thing: your honesty with yourself. No flattery loops. No engagement bait.

Privacy, briefly

Your reflections are yours. Your data is never sold, you can export or delete it anytime, and we are GDPR-compliant. Full details live in our Privacy Policy.
